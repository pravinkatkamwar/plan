Wife Details
If Wife Salary is $0, she is treated as a non-working wife — wife SS benefit (50% of your FRA benefit) applies and no 401k contributions are added. If salary > $0, her income and contributions are included in portfolio growth and joint MAGI (affects IRMAA).
Growth & Social Security
Wife SS is calculated as 50% of your FRA benefit (age-67 amount), reduced for each month claimed before her FRA (age 67). A non-working wife cannot earn more than this spousal benefit. · SS COLA: SS benefit grows each year by the COLA rate starting from the first year benefits are received. SSA historical average is ~2.5%.
Expenses
Annual gift reduces portfolio each year from Gift Start Age. 2024 annual exclusion is $18,000/person ($36k/yr to one child). Gift does not affect taxable income.
Tax & Conversion Strategy
Target Bracket: Each year the model converts just enough Trad 401k to fill taxable income up to the selected bracket ceiling. Adjust Roth Conv. Target ($) to fine-tune (e.g. set lower to avoid a higher IRMAA tier). · SS Taxable %: Up to 85% of SS is federally taxable for most retirees. · RMD Start Age: SECURE 2.0 sets this at 73; legislation may raise it to 75. · LTCG Rate: Used for reference — brokerage withdrawals are taxed at this rate on gains.
